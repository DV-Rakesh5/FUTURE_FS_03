Frequently Asked Questions

When can I visit?

Our gym is open Monday-Friday 6 AM - 10 PM, and Saturday-Sunday 7 AM - 9 PM. Premium and Elite members get 24/7 access.

Do you offer trials?

Yes! We offer a free 1-day trial pass or a low-cost 7-day trial week. Visit us or call to book your trial.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, all memberships can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice. No hidden fees or long-term contracts.

Do you have personal trainers?

Absolutely! Our certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one sessions and group training programs.

What equipment do you have?

We have a wide range of modern equipment including cardio machines, free weights, strength training equipment, and more.

Are classes included?

Group classes are included with Premium and Elite memberships. Basic members can add classes for a small fee.
